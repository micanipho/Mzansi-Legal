RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

Entered into between:

Rambo Properties CC (Hereinafter referred to as the "Landlord")

and

John Smith (Hereinafter referred to as the "Tenant")

1. Premises and Duration

1.1 The Landlord hereby leases to the Tenant the property located at Unit 42, Shady Pines Complex, Sandton.

1.2 The lease shall commence on 01 June 2026 and continue for a period of 12 months.

2. Rent and Payment

2.1 The monthly rental is R8,500.00, payable in advance on the 1st of every month.

2.2 If rent is not received by the 3rd of the month, the Landlord reserves the right to immediately disconnect the electricity and water supply to the premises without prior notice.

3. Deposit

3.1 A security deposit of R17,000.00 is required upon signing.

3.2 The Tenant agrees that the deposit will not be kept in an interest-bearing account. The Landlord retains the right to withhold the entire deposit at the end of the lease as an administrative convenience fee, regardless of the condition of the property.

4. Inspections

4.1 The Landlord or its agents may enter the properties at any time, day or night, without prior notice to the Tenant, to conduct inspections or show the property to prospective buyers.

5. Breach and Eviction

5.1 If the Tenant breaches any condition of this lease, including being one day late on rent, the lease is automatically cancelled.

5.2 In the event of cancellation under clause 5.1, the Landlord shall have the right to immediately change the locks, confiscate the Tenant's belongings, and forcefully evict the Tenant from the premises without the need to obtain a court order.

5.3 The Tenant expressly waives all their rights under the Prevention of Illegal Eviction from and Unlawful Occupation of Land Act 19 of 1998 (PIE Act) and the Rental Housing Act 50 of 1999.

6. Maintenance

6.1 The Tenant is entirely responsible for all maintenance, including structural repairs, roof leaks, and repairing the geyser, at their own expense.


Signed at Sandton on 15 May 2026
