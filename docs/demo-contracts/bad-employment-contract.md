# EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

**Entered into between:**
**Globex Logistics (Pty) Ltd** (Hereinafter referred to as the "Employer")
and
**Jane Doe** (Hereinafter referred to as the "Employee")

## 1. Commencment and Position

1.1 The Employee is appointed as a Warehouse Picker starting 01 May 2026.
1.2 The first 3 months of employment are considered a probationary period during which the Employer may terminate this contract with 24 hours' notice for any reason.

## 2. Remuneration

2.1 The Employee will be paid at a rate of R20.00 per hour, payable monthly.
2.2 If the Employee causes any damage to company property (including dropping stock), the Employer reserves the right to deduct the full replacement cost directly from the Employee's salary without prior consultation.

## 3. Working Hours and Overtime

3.1 The standard working hours are 50 hours per week (Monday to Saturday).
3.2 Overtime is mandatory when required by management. Overtime will be paid at the normal hourly rate of R20.00 per hour.
3.3 The Employee is entitled to a 20-minute lunch break per 9-hour shift.

## 4. Annual Leave and Sick Leave

4.1 The Employee is entitled to 10 days of paid annual leave per year.
4.2 Sick leave will only be paid if the Employee provides a medical certificate, even for a single day of absence.
4.3 Sick leave is limited to 15 days in a 3-year cycle.

## 5. Family Responsibility Leave

5.1 Family responsibility leave is not granted. Any absence due to family emergencies will be treated as unpaid leave.

## 6. Deductions

6.1 A monthly "uniform maintenance fee" of R250 will be deducted from the Employee's salary.

## 7. Termination

7.1 Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 1 week's written notice, regardless of the length of service.
7.2 The Employer may terminate this agreement immediately without a disciplinary hearing if the Employee is caught sleeping on duty.

**Signed at Johannesburg on 25 April 2026**
